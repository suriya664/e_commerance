Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full concierge rollout take?

Timelines range from 10 to 18 weeks depending on packaging production, logistics integrations, and custom design scope. We provide a detailed milestone map during discovery, ensuring each stakeholder has clarity.

Can we integrate with existing ERP or CRM platforms?

Absolutely. Our technology team supports Shopify Plus, Salesforce Commerce, Adobe Commerce, custom headless builds, and ERP integrations including Oracle and SAP to maintain continuity.

Do you manage international compliance and customs?

Yes. We coordinate with compliance partners across India and key export hubs. We prepare HS codes, sustainability reports, and VIP-specific customs handling to ensure seamless pan-India delivery.

How is success measured?

We align on KPIs such as conversion uplift, VIP retention, average order value, and packaging satisfaction scores. Dashboards provide real-time insights and quarterly recommendations.
