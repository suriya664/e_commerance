Phygital Runways: Crafting Couture Beyond Borders

By Saanvi Rao November 12, 2025

The aura of haute couture has expanded beyond the salon. With phygital runways, we design experiences where patrons in Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru witness a collection simultaneously. Each event includes synchronized lighting, scent diffusions, and immersive soundscapes tailored to each locale.

Our methodology centers around story arcs. Every chapter — Prelude, Reveal, and Encores — is supported by a digital twin environment. Collectors can request stylist consultations in real-time, bookmark looks, annotate details, and purchase through concierge chat with minimal friction.

“We ensure luxury isn’t compromised. The staging mirrors the intimacy of an atelier appointment, even when delivered through a digital channel to thousands of discerning viewers.”

We integrate 8K cinematic streaming, multi-angle cameras, and AR overlays that highlight craftsmanship. Each garment includes layered storytelling: sketches, atelier footage, and artisan interviews. The result is an emotional, data-rich experience that feels personal at scale across India.

Key Elements

  • Live stylists orchestrating on-screen wardrobes for each VIP viewer
  • Private salon rooms with synchronized music and lighting controls
  • Immediate concierge checkout and bespoke delivery scheduling
  • NFT certificates validating collectability and provenance for the future

After the encore, analytics dashboards summarize engagement, top looks per region, and styling requests. Designers receive immediate feedback, while VIP teams coordinate follow-up appointments within hours to maintain momentum.

We believe phygital runways will define the next decade of couture. The approach preserves heritage and craftsmanship while amplifying reach for modern maisons navigating a nationwide clientele.

About the Author

Saanvi Rao, Founder & Chief Curator of The Gild Store, has directed couture experiences for Indian luxury houses across Mumbai, Delhi, and Bengaluru, aligning storytelling with commerce outcomes.
